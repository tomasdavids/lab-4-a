# Scrambled Eggs

*A quick and easy breakfast ready in under 10 minutes*.

## Ingredients

1. 3 eggs
2. 2 tablespoons of milk
3.  tablespoon of butter
4.  Salt and pepper

## Steps

1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and add the milk.
2. Whisk everything together.
3. Melt the butter in a pan over low heat.
4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
5. Gently stir with a spatula until the eggs are just set.
6. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.
#(h1) Academic Integrity Policy

## Purpose

To maintain a high standard of academic integrity across the Seneca 
community and provide the foundation for research, teaching, learning, 
and working practices.

## Scope

This policy applies to current students and employees.

### Other Defined Terms

**Cheating** — Obtaining or attempting to get credit for work by 
dishonest or deceptive means.
**Contract Cheating** — When a student submits work created by a 
third party as their own.
**Plagiarism** — Using another person's work without properly 
citing the source.
**Self-Plagiarism** — The unauthorized re-use of one's own work 
from previous assignments.
